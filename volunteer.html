Volunteer

Join the support network.

Volunteers are a key part of the concept flow. This page explains how the platform expects volunteers to contribute and what skills are most useful.

Medical help

Doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and lab workers can support referrals and basic guidance.

Operations help

Drivers, logistics support, and coordinators can help move patients and supplies.

Tech / support

Students and software volunteers can help improve workflows, forms, and dashboards.

How to get involved

  • Fill the volunteer form in the portal.
  • Share your skills and availability clearly.
  • The team can then match you with appropriate support tasks.
